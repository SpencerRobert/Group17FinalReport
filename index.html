Redesign of the 'Reading Line' Chrome extension

By: Group 17
Mar 12, 2023

Executive Summary

The project we chose to work on throughout the course of the semester was a redesign of the Reading Line Chrome Extension.



Originally, this extension was very basic. When toggled on, it rendered a semi-transparent grey line that spanned the entire width of the user’s current web page. This line would follow the movement of the user’s cursor until it was toggled off, in which it then disapeared until re-enabled. It was simple but made for an effective aid in reading large blocks of text on webpages as it could assist the user in keeping track of the current line they were reading. Just like how it is being moved along this current block of text you are reading.
